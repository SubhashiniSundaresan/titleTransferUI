How Real Estate Ledger Works?

In the proposed process all the participants are given a unique ID and their details will be attached to that ID. Then the seller can list the property for sale which gets added to the asset registry. Buyer can go through the property listing and creates a sale agreement for the property that he/she is interested in. The proposed sale agreement can be verified by the seller and can sign the sale agreement. Once the sale agreement is signed by both the parties the tile can now be purchased and the notary creates a new deed for the property and approves the purchases

What We Offer

Each record in the ledger is time-stamped, is immutable and independently verifiable. Our blockchain secured data has provable integrity because the data is attributed to an irrefutable identity, is validated by all copies of the ledger, is always available, and can never be changed.

Key Takeaways

  • The blockchain enables real-time settlements of the recorded transactions.
  • The ledger is uncensored and the transactions are immutable.
  • The blockchain speeds up the process of title transfer and verified by using smart contracts.
  • Smart contracts can also help eliminate real estate fraud. False listings, forged documents and rental scams will all be nearly impossible when digital ownership is linked directly to a property on the blockchain.
  • Ensure transparency and authenticity in the assets we trade, purchase and sell globally
  • Protect the provenance of high value assets
